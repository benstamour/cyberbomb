Oh no! There's a bomb! Luckily, there's a DEFUSER on the scene to deactivate it. There's just one problem: they don't know how! The GUIDES have the instructions on how to defuse the bomb, but they can't see what it looks like.

The DEFUSER must tell the GUIDES what they can see on the bomb. The GUIDES respond by asking for further details and giving instructions to the DEFUSER on how to deactivate each module on the bomb.

For example, a DEFUSER may say that they see a group of wires on the bomb. The GUIDES may ask how many wires there are.

This game can be played with two or more people. One person is the DEFUSER, who can only see the bomb, while everyone else is a GUIDE, who can only see the manual. The players win if the DEFUSER successfully deactivates all of the modules on the bomb within the time limit.
